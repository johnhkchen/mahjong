## Intent — the bigger-picture play

Give the table its climax: a hand can now be won, and the win explains itself. Build the pure agari layer in core — decompose concealed tiles + melds into four sets and a pair, plus chiitoitsu and kokushi, and derive the waits of a tenpai hand — then widen HandAction with tsumo (active seat, drawn or rinshan tile) and ron (claimed through the existing E-004 claim seam, with ron priority above pon/kan above chi). A win ends the hand deterministically from the record, with winner, winning tile, and satisfied yaku recorded in TableState and any multiple-ron convention documented in core. Enforce the one-yaku win gate via a closed catalog of standard yaku predicates valid in this slice (menzen tsumo through chinitsu, chiitoitsu, haitei/houtei/rinshan, and the yakuman forms reachable without riichi), returning yaku NAMES only — han/fu valuation stays with the scoring epic. Apply basic discard furiten: a seat whose waits intersect its own pond gets no ron offer from legalActions. In the view, offer East a tsumo/ron prompt through the existing prompt seam and show a hand-end screen naming winner, winning tile, and yaku made — the first teaching moment the app delivers.

## Value to the design

P1: the finishable hand becomes a WINNABLE hand — draw/discard/calls now terminate in agari, the core of playability. P5: agari decomposition property-tested against a brute-force reference over random hands, waits correctness, positive-and-negative cases per yaku, furiten gating, yakuless-win refusal, and conservation/determinism with wins in play form the rigor exhibit this repo exists to demonstrate. P2 (seeded): the hand-end screen naming the yaku made is the first moment mahjong's scoring language becomes legible to a solitaire-tapper.

## Done looks like

A seeded hand can be played to a genuine win: East is offered tsumo or ron at the legal moment (and NOT offered ron when furiten or yakuless), the hand ends with winner, winning tile, and yaku names shown on the hand-end screen, replaying the same log reproduces the same win, and the property suite — agari-vs-brute-force, waits, the full yaku catalog positive and negative, furiten, yakuless refusal, conservation and determinism — is green.
