## Context

Stop interrupting for calls nobody would make: by default, a claim window prompts the
player only when a win is offered or when at least one claim passes the exported bot
policy judgment (shanten-cutting / yaku-preserving — reuse the policy's own call
evaluation, no new legality logic); filtered windows auto-pass through the existing
settleWindow seam. A persisted "prompt every legal call" toggle (one localStorage key,
terminology-toggle precedent, dictionary terms for its label) restores full prompting.

_Advances: P2, P4_

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] With the default setting, a window whose only claims the policy would decline
  never renders a prompt and play proceeds as a pass; wins and policy-approved claims
  always prompt; the toggle flips behavior live and survives reload; both
  terminologies covered. Interaction suite extends the E-011 fixtures rather than
  duplicating them.
