## Context

A freshly-mounted ClaimPrompt (and RiichiPrompt) ignores button activations during its
entry beat (~the existing CSS transition duration), so a tap or double-tap aimed at the
previous window can never land on the new one. Under `prefers-reduced-motion` the beat
collapses and the guard collapses with it (keep them one constant). Guard is
presentation-only: the buttons render immediately, activations inside the beat are
inert.

_Advances: P4, P2_

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Interaction tests (fake timers) prove: an activation inside the beat does
  nothing and the same activation after the beat works; the win button obeys the same
  beat and no more; existing E-011 suites updated to advance past the beat rather than
  weakening assertions. `just test` green.
