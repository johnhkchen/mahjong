## Context

Make the hand playable end-to-end: the app appends actions to its authoritative record — East discards by tapping a tile, the three other seats tsumogiri as the deliberate bot placeholder — with all legality consumed from legalActions, so a seeded hand runs from deal to exhaustive draw purely as a fold.

_Advances: P1_

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] In just dev a full seeded hand plays from deal to ryuukyoku by tapping tiles: taps only succeed on legally discardable tiles, non-East seats auto-tsumogiri, ponds and wall counter update after every action, and the ryuukyoku state appears exactly when the wall empties; an app test asserts the tap handler builds actions via legalActions rather than computing legality locally.
